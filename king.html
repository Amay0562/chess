The King:

This piece is one of the most valuble pieces in chess, because if you get checkmated the game is OVER and if someone checks you, you are not allowed to move any piece except your king, if you do you will do an illegal move, 2 illegal moves and you are out of the game

Location:

If the king is white it is Just Beside to the bishop on the green/black square, if the king is black it again be beside the bishop but it will be on the white square

Movement:

The king can move in all directions, but it can only move one square at a time
