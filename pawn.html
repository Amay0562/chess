The Pawns

This piece is called the "Pawn" a very small piece worth 1 point

Location:

The whole row ahead of all the previous pieces

Movement:

It can only move ahead by one square, can also move one step diagonally if there is another piece diagonally above by one square it can move two squares only IN THE STARTING

Example:

Promotion:

When a pawn reaches the other side of the board it can become any piece except itself and the king
